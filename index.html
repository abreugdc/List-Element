Magic Tea

Ingredients

Separating the ingredients

  • 800 ml of water
  • 2 pieces of cinnamon
  • 1/2 Onion in slices
  • 4~5 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 Spoon of Honey
  • 5 cloves from India
  • 2 Bags of Tea (any flavor)
  • 5 spoons of sugar

Preparation of ingredients

  • cut the lemon into 4 parts
  • crush or cut the garlic cloves into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients in a pan
  2. Mix with the help of a spoon
  3. Cover the pan and wait for the water to boil
  4. Keep it boiling for 3 minutes
  5. Turn off the heat and wait 10 minutes for the properties to increase to the tea.
  6. With the help of a sieve, separate the ingredients from the liquid
  7. Enjoy your magic tea!
